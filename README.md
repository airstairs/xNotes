tip: redo will replace accidentally erased strokes  
tip: tap swiping up and down will quick zoom when pencil tool selected you just have to undo the quick zoom confirm stroke afterwards similar to nova launchers tap swipe just dont let go at end of swipe  
tip: you can zoom in and out on the canvas regularly by pinching in and out with two fingers  
tip: you can move and navigate the canvas by panning (or scrolling) up down left and right with two fingers close together (pinching in and out during this will cancel and trigger zoom)  
tip: for best performance do zooming drawing and navigating all as separate and independant sequential manuevers
